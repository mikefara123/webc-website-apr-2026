LEGALPRIVACY POLICY

Privacy policy.

What we collect, how we use it, and how to get us to stop.

Last updated: 21 April 2026 · Plain English version. privacy@webcentriq.com

The short version.

You give us your email and a paragraph about your project, we use it to email you back an estimate, we follow up once. If you tell us to stop, we stop. We don't sell your data, we don't run ad tracking, and we keep third-party services to a minimum.

Who we are.

WebCentriq Inc. A software development studio, incorporated in the United States, with offices in San Diego, California and Markham, Ontario, Canada. You can reach our privacy contact at privacy@webcentriq.com.

This policy covers webcentriq.com and the AI estimator we run on it.

What we collect.

Only what you give us, plus what the server logs automatically.

From the estimator form

  • Your email address
  • Your country and city
  • A project description you type
  • Project type, optional budget and timeline signals
  • How you heard about us and how urgent it is

From your browser (automatic)

  • Your IP address (used only for rate limiting and security)
  • Your browser user-agent string
  • The page you came from (HTTP referrer), if any
  • Timestamps of your visit and form submission

What we don't collect

  • We don't use third-party advertising pixels, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar ad-tracking
  • We don't buy data about you from other sources
  • We don't collect payment information on this site — any invoicing is handled separately, under a separate agreement
  • We don't use session recording or heatmap tools

How we use it.

  • Generate your estimate. Your description, project type, budget signal, and timeline preference are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. The API returns a structured estimate. We do not fine-tune or train models on your data.
  • Email you the result. We send you the estimate. The email provider is Resend.
  • Notify our sales team. A senior engineer at WebCentriq reviews the estimate and decides whether to follow up. Your contact details and project description go to them so they can reply usefully.
  • Follow up once. Expect a human reply within 72 hours. If you don't reply, that's the end of it — we don't run follow-up drip campaigns.
  • Prevent abuse. Your IP address is used only for rate-limiting the API so no one can submit thousands of estimates in an afternoon.

Why we're allowed to process it (GDPR).

If you're in the EU or UK, our legal bases are:

  • Your consent — clicking Submit on the estimator form gives us explicit consent to process the information you typed. You can withdraw at any time (see "Your rights" below).
  • Legitimate interests — for basic server-log / anti-abuse data. We've weighed our interest in running a functioning website against your privacy and think this is proportionate.

Who else sees your data.

Four parties, all processors acting on our behalf:

PartyPurposeLocation
Anthropic Runs the Claude AI model that generates the estimate United States
Resend Sends the estimate email and the internal notification United States
Vercel Hosts the website and serverless functions United States
Google Fonts Serves the web fonts (no cookies, but your IP reaches Google) United States

We don't sell your data. We never have. We never will.

International transfers.

All of our processors are based in the United States. If you're in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction with cross-border transfer rules, your data is transferred to the U.S. under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in our contracts with these vendors.

How long we keep it.

  • Active lead. As long as there's an open conversation with you about a project, plus 12 months after the last reply.
  • Closed lead. 24 months after the last contact.
  • Server logs. 30 days, then deleted automatically by Vercel.
  • Estimate email. Resend retains transactional emails for 30 days then purges.

You can ask us to delete earlier. See below.

Your rights.

Depending on where you live, you have some combination of:

  • The right to see what we have. Ask us, we'll send you a copy.
  • The right to correct it. If something's wrong, we'll fix it.
  • The right to delete it. We'll wipe the record and confirm when done.
  • The right to restrict processing. You can tell us to keep your data but not use it.
  • The right to port it. We'll export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • The right to opt out of "sale". We don't sell data — so there's nothing to opt out of, but you have the right anyway (California CCPA).
  • The right to withdraw consent. Email us and we stop processing.

To exercise any of these: email privacy@webcentriq.com. We'll respond within 30 days (usually much faster).

If we get it wrong, you can complain to your local data protection authority. In the EU that's your national DPA; in the UK it's the ICO; in California it's the CPPA.

Cookies.

We use the smallest cookie footprint we can get away with.

CookiePurposeDurationFirst-party?
wq_consent_v1 (localStorage, not a cookie technically) Remembers your cookie-notice choice so we stop showing the banner Until you clear it Yes

That's the entire list today. No tracking cookies. No analytics cookies. No ad pixels. No session cookies.

If we add web analytics later (probably Plausible or Vercel Analytics, both of which claim cookie-less operation), we'll update this policy and ask for your consent before turning it on.

Children.

This site isn't directed at children under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you think a child has submitted the estimator, email us and we'll delete their record.

Security.

The estimator form runs over HTTPS. Our serverless functions enforce rate limits, honeypot checks, and input validation. Database-level data (our CRM with lead info) is encrypted at rest and access is limited to the engineers and sales people who need it. We don't promise zero breaches — no one who's honest can — but we promise to tell you quickly if something happens that affects you.

Changes to this policy.

We'll update this page when we materially change how we handle your data. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. If we make a material change that affects you, we'll email you.

Questions.

Email privacy@webcentriq.com. A human will reply.
